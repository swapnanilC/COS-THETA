ABOUT US

We are COS THETA, an alternative rock band that thrives on emotion, intensity, and musical storytelling. Formed out of a shared love for powerful melodies and thought-provoking lyrics, our sound fuses gritty guitar work, haunting violin layers, dynamic rhythms, and expressive vocals. Every track we create is a reflection of our personal experiences, passions, and perspectives — a blend of rebellion and vulnerability. Our music bridges the gap between the raw energy of rock and the atmospheric depth of alternative influences. Whether it's a hard-hitting anthem or a soul-stirring ballad, we aim to connect with listeners on a deeper level. COS THETA is not just about the sound — it's about the feeling it leaves behind. We are not here to follow trends. We are here to create a sound that is uniquely ours — and share it with the world, one stage at a time.
